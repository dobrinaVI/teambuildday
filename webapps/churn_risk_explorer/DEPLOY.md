This repository includes the Streamlit app source in `webapps/churn_risk_explorer/app.py`.

In DSS (project `TEAMDAY_PAIR_01_A`), create/update a **STREAMLIT** webapp and paste the content of `app.py` into the webapp code.

Datasets expected by default:
- `customer_churn_scored` (scored + `explanations` JSON column)
- `customer_churn_scored_tenure12` (same, but with tenure forced to 12 months)
